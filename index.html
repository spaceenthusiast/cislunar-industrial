Cislunar Industrial

Heavy industry in cislunar space — large-scale mining, material processing, manufacturing, and construction operations utilizing lunar resources to build infrastructure, produce propellant, and support the expanding lunar economy.

Cislunar industrial activities reduce launch costs from Earth, enable self-sustaining bases, and create new supply chains for habitats, vehicles, and deep space missions under the Artemis framework and commercial initiatives.
